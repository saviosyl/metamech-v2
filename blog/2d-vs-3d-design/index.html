2D vs 3D Design: When Each Matters

2D and 3D design are not competitors — they are complementary. Understanding when to use each avoids wasted effort.

When 3D Is Essential

  • Complex assemblies
  • Motion and interference checks
  • Automation and equipment design

When 2D Still Wins

  • Simple fabrication parts
  • Clear communication to suppliers
  • Fast modifications

The Best Approach

Use 3D to design and validate. Use 2D to manufacture and communicate.
